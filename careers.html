Rec Room Values
We live by these philosophies.
We take a bias towards action. When in doubt, we build it. We value doers over talkers. Playable code speaks louder than any argument.
We ship and iterate constantly. We learn by trying new things and listening to our community.
We respect our customers. We value long term connections over making a quick buck. No exploitive monetization methods.
We are a diverse group of talented folks
Rec Room is a place to build, create and play games with friends. We believe “it's a small world” and are at our best when every member of our organization feels supported, respected, included, and heard. We welcome employees from all walks of life, from diverse racial and ethnic backgrounds, and all gender identities, sexual orientations, age groups, disability statuses, veteran status, and religious beliefs. Employees are free to be who they are, knowing they will be heard & supported.
Meet The Teams
At Rec Room, we encourage you to do what you love.
Art
The Art team creates and maintains Rec Room's visual identity and aesthetic. An artist at Rec Room gets to work broadly across many disciplines.
Open Roles
Data
The team’s mission is to use data to be an advocate for our players. You’ll develop a deep understanding of our ecosystem and user behavioral patterns to help us stay focused on building the platform that players want.
Open Roles
Economy
The Economy team is focused on bringing money in from players, paying money out to players, and all the steps in between. We work with the art team to design all the items we sell to players.
Open Roles
Experience
The Experience team is responsible for expanding cross-platform player capabilities, building awesome experiences to showcase them, and improving the player experience of Rec Room along the way.
Open Roles
Finance
The Finance team is responsible for our financial reporting, budgeting, forecasting and analysis, cash flow, treasury management, tax reporting, internal controls, and the annual audit.
Open Roles
Infrastructure
The Infrastructure team is in charge of proactively building and implementing services to make RecRoom teams better at their jobs. This includes infrastructure automation, monitoring and alerting, security, and IT support.
Open Roles
Growth
The Growth team is focused on customer lifecycle management through the entire user journey and across all user segments.
Open Roles
People Operations
The People Operations team’s areas of ownership include recruiting, onboarding, human resources, and office management.
Open Roles
Platform
The Platform team is responsible for getting Rec Room running on new hardware and software platforms as well as making sure it runs great on our current platforms.
Open Roles
Publishing
The Publishing team is focused on growth through strategic partnerships and major in-game brand or event integrations.
Open Roles
Quality Assurance
The QA team helps our developers find and prevent problems, or work with the community when we miss them. We own the testing and weekly shipping processes.
Open Roles
Server
The Server team builds the scalable backend services that power the online Rec Room universe, enabling everything from user-generated content sharing, to clubs and beyond.
Open Roles
Multiplayer
The Multiplayer team builds the real time network underpinnings that allow the Rec Room multiplayer experience to be a believable, stable, and scalable simulation for our players around the world. We work at multiple levels, from low level networking code and distributed state replication to orchestrating live 24/7 backend game services across the globe.
Open Roles
Social
On the Social team we empower players to make meaningful lasting friendships. We connect players to the content and creators they love. We cultivate these relationships to create a fun and welcoming experience for people from all walks of life.
Open Roles
Tools
The Tools team automates workflows, extends pipelines, and builds systems that make the rest of Rec Room as productive and disaster-proofed as possible. If you like putting smiles on your coworkers' faces, you might be a great fit for our Tools team!
Open Roles
User Generated Content
We ship and support creation tools that allow a growing community of creators to have fun building together and sharing awesome stuff! We want creators to love Rec Room.
